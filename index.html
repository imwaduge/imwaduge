About me

Hi there, my name is Ishan Madusanka Waduge and I'm currently pursuing a Bachelor of Information Communication Technology (Hons) degree with a specialization in Multimedia Technology at the Faculty of Technology, University of Sri Jayewardenepura. I have a passion for combining creative design and technical expertise to develop innovative solutions that solve real-world problems.

Throughout my studies, I have developed a strong foundation in branding, UI/UX design, graphic design, web design, video production, and programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JS, C#, PHP, MySQL, Java, and React JS. I am constantly seeking to expand my skills and knowledge, and I'm eager to apply my expertise to new challenges and projects.

In addition to my skills, I also have communication and strong collaboration abilities. I enjoy working in team environments and thrive when collaborating with others to achieve shared goals. I am committed to delivering high-quality work that exceeds expectations and I'm always looking for opportunities to learn and grow as a professional.

Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy Travel, Music, and Drawing. These hobbies keep me grounded and inspire me to think creatively and approach problem-solving from different angles.

Thank you for taking the time to read a little bit more about me. I would be delighted if there is an opportunity to contribute my skills and knowledge to your organization and be part of a team dedicated to growth and success.
